VELOCIRAPTOR

The Velociraptor was one of the most ferocious and most dangerous animal in the world.

They had the agility of a cheetah, the lethal touch of a tiger, and the instincts of a hawk.

With razor sharp teeth, claws on both arms, and a large talon-like hook on each foot, raptors were not to be messed with!
